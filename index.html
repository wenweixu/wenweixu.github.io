Birth control ? Yes please.

Unwanted pregnancies for a dog or a cat not only have high associated costs for termination or prenatal care and puppy/kitty placement, they can also make your dog or cat miserable and at risk for complications. Please don't do that to them.

If you are ready to make an appointment to get your dog or cat some birth control, check our resource page.
